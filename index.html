About Us

Green Edge Mowing was founded by two local teenagers, Jake and Thomas, who wanted to make it easier - and more affordable - for people in their community to keep their yards looking great. What started as a simple idea between two mates has grown into a reliable, youth-powered mowing service built on hard work, honesty, and genuine community spirit.

We're committed to showing that young people can deliver real value. We show up on time, work hard, and take pride in every lawn we mow and every edge we sharpen. Our goal is to provide a service that's accessible, dependable, and high quality.

Green Edge Mowing isn't just a business - it's a way for us to learn, grow, and give back. When you support us, you're supporting motivated young locals who care about their work and their community.

Your lawn gets the edge. Your community gets stronger.

Availability
